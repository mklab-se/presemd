# Speaker Notes

A demonstration of the `???` separator

## Why Speaker Notes?

- Help presenters understand slide intent
- Provide delivery guidance and talking points
- Essential for AI-generated presentations
+ Enable collaboration between slide creator and presenter

???

Walk through each bullet point. The key insight is the last one:
when MDeck creates a presentation with AI, every slide gets detailed
notes explaining **what** to say and **how** to present it.

Pause after revealing the last bullet — let it sink in.

---

## How to Add Notes

Use `???` (three question marks) as a separator:

```markdown
# My Slide

- Content here

???

Your notes go here. They support
**bold**, *italic*, and `code`.
```

???

Show the code block and explain that the `???` separator works
just like `---` for slide breaks and `+++` for column separators.

Mention that `????` (four or more) also works as a notes separator.

---

## Notes in AI-Generated Presentations

When MDeck creates a presentation with `mdeck ai create`:

- Every slide gets detailed speaker notes
- Notes explain the slide's purpose and intent
- Suggested talking points are included
+ The presenter can deliver effectively even without reading the source material

???

This is the big selling point. Emphasize that a presenter who
has never seen the original content can still deliver the talk
effectively, because the AI-generated notes explain everything.

Ask the audience: "How many times have you been handed a slide
deck with no context about what to say?"

---

## Best Practices

- Keep notes concise but instructive
- Include the "why" behind each slide
- Suggest questions to ask the audience
- Note any pauses or emphasis points
- Include timing hints for long presentations

???

Run through the best practices quickly. These are guidelines, not rules.

The most important one is the "why" — if the presenter understands
the intention behind a slide, they can adapt their delivery to
the specific audience and context.
